Robotics

I did Robotics for three years! I learned more than I could have ever imagined and it helped make me into who I am today!

I was just starting out, exploring all the aspects of the FLL Robotics program. My team and I had to build attachments for the robots with legos and program them to do specific tasks! I gained knowledge about how weight, size, leverage, motors, structures and how to problem-solve.

Year 2

This year, I added the Project part of the Robotics program. We had to research and invent a solution to a real-world problem today! I learned how to research efficiently, interview proffesionals, organize my data, create models and diagrams, presentation skills, speaking, and countless others.

Year 3

My third year in Robotics, we also focused on Core Values which includes leadership, teamwork, time-management, creativity and more. These skills are important to master because they will be used life-long. Also, I learned how to mentor and help others effectivly. We won many awards this year!
